Collecta - VolleyBall Collecting Robot

Automating the manual efforts and time required by athletes to collect volleyballs indoors

Find Out More

Our Solution to BallSports

Athletes spend alot of time and effort after practice to collect and organize loose volleyballs. Our FYDP aims to solve this problem through automation.

Check Out Our Robot!

COLLECTA

Top Speed

The robot is expected to reach top speeds of 2 m/s. That means it can traverse an entire volleyball court in under 5 seconds.

Intelligent Ball Detection and Path Planning

State of the art machine learning algorithms are used to detect and plan the optimal path to collect volleyballs.

Lightweight and Portable

Weighing less than a standard piece of luggage, Collecta can be moved and used in any indoor volleyball court.

Quick Ball Retrieval and Storage

Collecta collects balls at a fast rate upto 3 volleyballs per run.

DETAILED DESIGN

Robot Chassis
Collecta features a lightweight and modular aluminium extrusion frame.
Detection System
Collecta features a kinect camera for volleyball detection and path planning.
Ball Retrieval
Collecta has a capacity of 3 volleyball with collection cycle time of 1 second. Balls are released from the inclined storage system through the back gate.
